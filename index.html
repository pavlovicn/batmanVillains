Two-Face

Harvey Dent

"Created by Bob Kane and Bill Finger. First appearance: Detective Comics #66 (August 1942). Abilities: Criminal mastermind, skilled marksman and expert lawyer and tactician."

Ra's al Ghul

"Created by Dennis O'Neil, Neal Adams and Julius Schwartz. First appearance: Batman #232 (June 1971). Abilities: Criminal and terrorist mastermind, skilled martial artist and hand-to-hand combatant, skilled alchemist and swordsman and longevity from Lazarus Pits"

Mister Freeze

Victor Fries

"Created by Dave Wood and Sheldon Moldoff. First appearance: Batman #121 (February 1959). Abilities: Superhuman intelligence, sub-zero physiology allows survival at a frozen state, wears refrigerated exo-skeletal suit granting superhuman strength and durability and utilizes cryogenic weaponry."

Killer Croc

Waylon Jones

"Created by Gerry Conway and Gene Colan. First appearance: Detective Comics #523 (February 1983). Abilities: Enhanced strength, speed, agility, reflexes, stamina, senses, and durability, reptilian teeth, claws, and scale-like skin and accelerated healing."

Joker

"Created by Jerry Robinson, Bill Finger and Bob Kane. First appearance: Batman #1 (April 25, 1940). Abilities: Criminal mastermind, expert chemist, utilizes weaponized props and toxins. True name has never been revealed."

Harley Quinn

Harleen Quinzel

"Created by Paul Dini and Bruce Timm. First appearance in "Joker's Favor", the twenty-second episode of Batman: The Animated Series. Abilities: Expert gymnast, immunity to various toxins, utilizes weaponized props, genius level intellect and trained in psychiatry."

Riddler

Edward Nygma

"Created by Bill Finger and Dick Sprang. First appearance: Detective Comics #140 (October 1948). Abilities: Genius-level intellect, expert criminal mastermind with a compulsive need for attention, skilled engineer, computer programmer, and escape artist and utilizes complex riddles, puzzles, and lethal deathtraps."

Clayface

Basil Karlo

"Created by Bob Kane and Bill Finger. First appearance: Detective Comics #40 (June 1940). Abilities: Shapeshifting, enhanced strength, endurance, and durability and duplication."

Scarecrow

Dr. Jonathan Crane

"Created by Bob Kane and Bill Finger. First appearance: World's Finest Comics #3 (September 1941). Abilities: Expert in psychology and biochemistry, skilled in the use of a scythe and utilizes fear-inducing drugs and toxins."

Bane

"Created by Dennis O'Neil, Chuck Dixon and Graham Nolan. First appearance: Batman: Vengeance of Bane #1 (January 1993). Abilities: Genius-level tactician, master hand-to-hand combatant and martial artist and superhuman strength and physical attributes via the Venom drug."

Penguin

Oswald Cobblepot

"Created by Bob Kane and Bill Finger. First appearance: Detective Comics #58 (December 1941). Abilities: Criminal mastermind, utilizes weaponized umbrellas and other equipment, assorted bird-related and bird expert, exceptional intelligence, master tactician and toxicologist."

Poison Ivy

Pamela Isley

"Created by Robert Kanigher and Sheldon Moldoff. First appearance: Batman #181 (June 1966). Abilities: Trained botanist, secretion of floral toxins and mind controlling pheromones, immunity to toxins and pathogens and plant manipulation via connection to the Green."

Court of Owls

"Created by Scott Snyder and Greg Capullo. First appearance: Batman Vol. 2 #3 (October 2011). The Court of Owls is an organized crime group and secret society."

Man-Bat

Kirk Langstrom

"Created by Frank Robbins, Neal Adams and Julius Schwartz. First appearance: Detective Comics #400 (June 1970). Abilities: Superhuman strength, flight, sharp claws, echolocation and sound manipulation."

Deadshot

Floyd Lawton

"Created by David Vern Reed and Lew Sayre Schwartz. First appearance: Batman #59 (June 1950). Abilities: Master Marksman, master assassin, expert combatant and expert tactician."

Catwoman

Selina Kyle

"Created by Jerry Robinson, Bill Finger and Bob Kane. First appearance: Batman #1 (April 25, 1940). Abilities: expert burglar, skilled hand-to-hand combatant, skilled gymnast and utilizes bullwhips, sharp retractable claws, and climbing pitons."

Mad Hatter

Jervis Tetch

"Created by Bill Finger and Lew Sayre Schwartz. First appearance: Batman #49 (October 1948). Abilities: Genius-level intellect, trained neuroscientist and utilizes mind-control technology."
